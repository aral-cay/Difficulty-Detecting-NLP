## üí° Tips for Creating Content

### For Beginner Level:
- Start with "What is..."
- Use simple, everyday language
- Provide context and examples
- Focus on definitions

### For Intermediate Level:
- Use "How does..." or "What is the difference..."
- Connect related concepts
- Show processes and applications
- Compare and contrast

### For Advanced Level:
- Use "Explain the theory..." or "How does X architecture..."
- Include technical terminology
- Discuss mathematical foundations
- Synthesize multiple complex concepts
